Lighting for Mood: The Single-Source Setup

MA
Dr. Mohamed Asser
Director of Photography · 24 films

How a single light source — a window, a lamp, a candle — can carry the entire emotional weight of a scene. We break down the single-source setup used in classic noir and contemporary MENA cinema, then rebuild it on a zero-budget kit.

What you'll be able to do

◆Shape mood with one light and a bounce
◆Control falloff using inverse-square distance
◆Read a face for short vs. broad lighting
◆Motivate a key light inside the frame
