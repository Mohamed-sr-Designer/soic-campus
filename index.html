One platform replaces them all

Your entire journey lives inside SOIC.

Today, learning is scattered across a dozen apps. We pulled it into one cinematic campus — so nothing, and no one, gets lost.

  • Cinematic learning

    Video lessons with transcripts, notes, AI summaries, and your next step always queued.

  • Live classrooms

    HD sessions with raise-hand, polls, breakout crews, attendance, and instant replays.

  • A real community

    Spaces, channels, voice rooms, and crews — belonging that Discord and WhatsApp can't give a film school.

  • Auto-built portfolio

    Every assignment becomes a portfolio piece on your own public film profile.
